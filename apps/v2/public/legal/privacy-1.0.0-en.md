# Privacy Policy

Novalis Inc. (the "Company") hereby establishes the following Privacy Policy regarding the handling of users' personal information in the service "Novalis" (the "Service") provided by the Company.

## Article 1. Definition of Personal Information

In this Policy, "personal information" refers to "personal information" as defined in the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (the Personal Information Protection Act): information about a living individual that can identify that specific individual through name, date of birth, or other descriptions contained therein.

## Article 2. Collection of Personal Information

When a user registers for the Service, the Company collects the following information:

- Name, email address, date of birth, nationality, and place of residence (prefecture)
- Basic profile information obtained via Facebook login
- Access logs and operation history during use of the Service
- Information provided by the user when making inquiries

## Article 3. Purposes of Collection and Use

The Company collects and uses personal information for the following purposes:

- To provide and operate the Service
- To respond to user inquiries
- To send important notices such as maintenance and changes to terms
- To respond to violations of the Terms of Service
- To analyze and improve usage of the Service
- To deliver new features and campaign information (only with consent)

## Article 4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without user consent, except in the following cases:

- When required by law
- When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property
- When particularly necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound development of children
- When cooperation with a state organ or similar entity is required

## Article 5. Outsourcing

The Company may outsource all or part of the handling of personal information within the scope necessary to achieve the purposes of use. Appropriate contracts including confidentiality obligations are concluded with outsourcing providers.

Main outsourcing providers:

- Supabase (PostgreSQL database — US, EU, Asia regions)
- Vercel (application hosting — US)
- Komoju / DEGICA (payment processing — Japan)
- Google (Gemini API — AI processing — US, Asia)
- Meta Platforms (Facebook Messenger integration — US)
- Sentry (error monitoring — US)

## Article 6. Retention Period

Personal information will be deleted within 30 days of withdrawal or a deletion request.

However, information that is required to be retained by law or that is necessary for investigating unauthorized use will continue to be retained only for the necessary period.

Consent logs for the Terms of Service will be retained as legal evidence even after a deletion request.

## Article 7. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion of Personal Information

Users may request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, or suspension of provision to third parties of their personal information held by the Company.

Requests should be sent to ryuhei.miyasawa@novalisgroup.biz. After identity verification, the Company will respond within a reasonable period.

## Article 8. Use of Cookies

The Service uses cookies to improve the user experience.

Users can disable cookies through browser settings, but some features may become unavailable as a result.

## Article 9. Security

The Company implements the following measures to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of personal information:

- Encryption of communication channels (TLS)
- Database access control (row-level security)
- Encrypted storage of confidential information
- Periodic security audits
- Employee training and acquisition of confidentiality pledges

## Article 10. Handling of Minors

The Service is intended for persons aged 18 or older.

If a user is found to be under the age of 18, the Company will cancel that user's registration and promptly delete their personal information.

## Article 11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

The contents of this Policy may be changed without notifying users, except for matters provided otherwise by law or in this Policy.

The revised Policy will take effect from the time it is posted on this site.

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact:
